AI-Powered Analysis
About this AI Feature

This AI combines its general knowledge (to understand language) with the live data provided from this dashboard to answer your questions.

It is strictly instructed to NOT use any external facts or data in its answer.

Its answers are based exclusively on:

  • Population structure (by age)
  • Dependency Ratios
  • Growth trends (2015-2030)
  • National averages for comparison

If you ask about other topics (e.g., unemployment, poverty), it will state that the information is not available.

AI Policy Insight
What is 'AI Policy Insight'?

Details: An automated analysis that highlights the primary demographic challenge or opportunity for the selected district. It's designed to frame the complex data profile into a single, clear, and policy-focused statement.

How it Works: This insight is not generated live. Instead, key metrics for each district were fed to a Generative AI model (Google's Gemini). The AI's single-sentence analysis was then captured and stored in the ai_insight property of the GeoJSON file.

Dependency Ratio

What is 'Dependency Ratio'?

Calculation: (Total Youth + Total Elderly) ÷ Total Working-Age × 100

Interpretation: A ratio of 60% means there are 60 "dependent" people (Youth & Elderly) for every 100 people of Working-Age.

In short: A key metric measuring the economic pressure on the productive population. A lower ratio is generally more favorable.

AI Trend Filter
What is 'AI Trend Classification'?

Details: These tags (e.g., "Aging", "Youth Bulge") are pre-calculated. Each district is analyzed and classified based on a statistical model of its demographic share (% of population) and growth rate (2015-2030) for key age groups.

In short: This filter instantly highlights all districts on the map that share the same significant demographic profile.
